About

I am a data scientist working at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and healthcare. My research focuses on extracting structured medical knowledge from unstructured clinical text and building robust AI systems for clinical decision support.

I hold a PhD in Computer Science, where I worked on biomedical event extraction, combining machine learning and linguistic knowledge. I am particularly interested in moving beyond traditional NER towards richer semantic representations of medical data.
